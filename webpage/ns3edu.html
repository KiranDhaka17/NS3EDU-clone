About

We Started as
Networking & Security Tech Experts

NS3TechSolutions is the parent organisation of NS3Edu and as a Tech solution brand we have had our market presence for 5+ years now. During our journey we have served 100+ partners with a 1000+ satisfied Customer base around the globe. Our Expertise lies in delivering all types of efficient networking solutions (managed, advanced and professional) to all small, medium and large scale enterprises. We have a vision to provide best technology consulting and system integration services based on organisation needs with a special focus on cost optimzation. We are also known for delivering corporate training and till date we have successfully delivered 50+ trainings with customised batch size and flexible training schedules.

Vision & Mission Behind
           NS3Edu

While onboarding beginners and experienced professionals at NS3Techsolutions, our founders identified that the hired professionals were facing gaps between the theoretical networking fundamentals and its practical applications. So regular training sessions were being delivered to our workforce. So to bridge the gap between knowledge of Cisco certifications and its application in the real-time industry. The foundation of Ns3Edu was laid in a separate branch.


The mission of Ns3Edu is to empower our candidate with in-depth knowledge of networking fundamentals along with real-time industry experience and also take 100% responsibility for its placement assistance post certification completion.
